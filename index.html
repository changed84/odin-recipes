Lasagna Recipe

While there are a few steps to this lasagna recipe, it’s easy to make and has so much flavor.
This dish can be made ahead of time and freezes well either before or after baking!

Ingredients

  • Lasagna pasta
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomato Paste
  • Parsley
  • Meat
  • Onion Powder
  • Ricotta

Steps

  1. Brown your beef
  2. Prep your veggies
  3. Make your cheese sauce
  4. Assemble your lasagna
  5. Bake
  6. Enjoy!


Caesar Salad Recipe

The crowd-pleasing salad of crisp romaine leaves, crunchy croutons, and a little or a lot of anchovy, as you like.

Ingredients

  • Romaine Lettuce
  • Olive Oil
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Hearty Bread
  • Anchovies
  • Salt & Pepper
  • Worcestershire Sauce

Steps

  1. Mix your dressing
  2. Prep your veggies
  3. Make breadcrumbs
  4. Toss the salad
  5. Enjoy!
